# Environment Setup Guide

This workshop is designed to be fully analog to maximize engagement and minimize technical friction [Source: tmpecbsbydx.docx].

## Prerequisites
- **Hardware:** None required. No laptops or digital devices are needed for the exercises.
- **Space:** A large meeting room with at least 3-4 whiteboards and tables arranged for breakout groups.
- **Materials:** 
  - Sticky notes (multiple colors).
  - Fine-tip markers for all participants.
  - Printed reference cards (Scrum Roles, Kanban Principles).

## Setup Steps
1. **Room Layout:** Arrange tables in pods of 4-6 to facilitate group work.
2. **Stationing:** Place a whiteboard and a pack of markers at each pod.
3. **Materials Distribution:** Place reference cards at each seat before the start time.

## Troubleshooting
- **Low Participation:** Use the 'Gallery Walk' to force movement and interaction.
- **Tech Dependency:** If participants bring laptops, kindly ask them to close them to maintain focus on the physical board design [Source: tmpecbsbydx.docx].

## Offline Fallback
Since the workshop is 100% analog, there are no digital dependencies to fail. Ensure extra markers are available in case of ink depletion.
