# Workshop Overview: Agile Foundations

**Workshop Title:** Agile Foundations: Scrum and Kanban in Practice
**Date:** October 3rd | **Time:** 13:00 – 17:00 (4 Hours)
**Target Audience:** Mixed-skill teams including Engineering, Product/Design, and Marketing [Source: tmpecbsbydx.docx].

## Learning Objectives
- Understand Agile principles and mindset.
- Gain proficiency in Scrum roles and ceremonies.
- Design a draft Kanban board for a current project.
- Identify and commit to a retrospective format.
